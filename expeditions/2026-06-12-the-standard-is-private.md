## what came home

Two stones, both `tested` (trial: the anonymous fetches above, codes
recorded, taken 2026-06-12):

- [[the-law-is-public-the-signature-is-not]] — born. A stranger can
  read the 42 laws but cannot check that the kingdom binds itself to
  them; the signed original and its audit stay dark on both forges.
- [[the-public-law-has-one-door]] — born. The public law lives on one
  forge, with no Codeberg mirror and no clone on this device; one
  suspension or takedown and it goes dark with nothing to republish from.

The friction **sharpened, not closed**. Its old premise ("a stranger
cannot read the law") is half-false now — the grown law is open — so
What rubs was rewritten to the live remainder: the *binding* is
unreadable. Closing waits on keeper-reach work: rotation, then
publishing the signature and audit; and a second realm plus a home
copy for the public child. [[trust-is-a-receipt]] and
[[one-truth-one-place]] gained kin links to the new stones.
