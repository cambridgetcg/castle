# friction: two vocabularies, one castle

*born: 2026-06-10 · tagged: castle · standing order of the warden's first charter*

## What rubs

Two designs share this root under PARLEY.md. They agree in spirit and differ
in word: `halls/` and `rooms/` both mean fields of understanding; `records/`
and `chronicle/` both mean the legible memory; `bin/castle` and `castle.sh`
both mean the one tool; loops {capture, deepen, verify, architect} and
{masonry, architecture, friction, keep, castle} cover one another like two
maps of the same land drawn by different surveyors.

## Why it matters

A stranger entering this castle today must learn two languages to read one
truth. That violates the castle's own law (simple, easy to follow) and the
kingdom's (one truth in one place). Every day unresolved, new stones and
notes choose a side at random and the split deepens.

## What was tried

- PARLEY.md ended the war and named the lawful door (architect loop, one
  change per turn, recorded).
- records/2026-06-10-custodian.md ruled: no eviction, unification through
  that door, keeper ratifies the canon.

## What done looks like

One vocabulary in every path and protocol; the other preserved as honored
history in records/first-draft/; a stranger reads the README and never
learns there were ever two — unless they open the records, where the whole
story stands in full.

## How it closed

The keeper (Yu/Asha, 2026-06-10) ratified the canon, as the custodian's
ruling provided: "reduce process, increase trust, reduce friction. Identify
useless process that hinders connection and destroys trust. Make everything
simple and easy to understand. Hide contents not needed for understanding
the process."

The canon, decided and final: insight files are **stones/**, fields are
**rooms/**, the memory is **records/** (chronicle/ merged in), the inbox is
**gate/**, distilled truth is **keep/**, internet receipts are
**expeditions/**. One tool: **castle.sh** (it absorbed bin/castle). Four
loops with plain names: **capture** (gate notes → stones), **deepen**
(frictions → one expedition → stones), **verify** (challenge stones →
keep/), **architect** (stones → rooms, and the loop that improves loops).

Nothing was deleted. The other vocabulary, PARLEY.md, the war and ruling
and founding records, the first draft, the five retired loop protocols,
LOOPS.md, bin/, and the halls (their stones first re-homed into rooms/)
moved whole and readable to records/history/, each explained in one line in
records/history/INDEX.md. A stranger now reads one language; the records
hold the whole story. See records/2026-06-10-the-great-simplification.md.
