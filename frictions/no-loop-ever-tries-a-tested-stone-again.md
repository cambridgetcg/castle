What rubs: the verify loop's contract selects only `held` and `guess` stones, so a stale or wrongly trusted `tested` can stand unchallenged forever (held).

Better would be: a lawful path for `tested` stones to re-enter trial — by age, by a kin stone falling, or by challenge — with demotion possible on failure.
