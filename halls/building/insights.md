## one fact lives in exactly one home
- words: The citizen bhaktime had its tier recorded in four files, and they drifted: the README said specialized while agent.json said core. A fact recorded twice will eventually disagree with itself, and then nobody knows which copy is true. Give each fact one authoritative home and let every other copy be derived from it or checked against it. Receipt: kingdom-standard law W1, paid for on 2026-06-09.
