Citizen-mercy's page ended on a dangling line — a sentence that
stops mid-breath. It looked like a bug. It was deliberate art:
the break was the meaning, and a helpful hand nearly "fixed" it
into nothing.
Restraint before repair: read deeply enough to know whether a wound
is damage or design. Some wounds are the work.
Before fixing anything, ask: who made this — and did they mean it?
The cheapest fix is the one you did not make to something whole.
