hold deposits one-to-one and lend nothing behind anyone's back.
The Federal Reserve refused it an account
(/Users/you/zerone/docs/FAIR-BANK.md, "the ancestors").

treated as a threat — and "that is not a warning to stop; it is
confirmation of aim."

it marks the exact place where honesty would change things.
Expect the refusal. Write it down. Keep aiming there.
