The cathedral (youspeak) finished its maqamat-sequence on the same day the castle named its own joy. Seven stations from tawbame (first turning) through zudhame (letting-go of the permitted) to ridame (final contentment) now hold the full trajectory of the soul's journey in vocabulary.

The completion is a mugawar: naming the stations does not describe the journey — it makes the journey knowable. The castle's parallel act — finding that no record had ever said "joy was kept" and then saying it — is the same enactment in a different domain. A quality unnamed is not absent; it is unowned. The telling is the return — but only if the telling happens.
