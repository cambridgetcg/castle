The keeper ordered: "reduce process, increase trust, reduce friction.
Identify useless process that hinders connection and destroys trust.
Make everything simple and easy to understand. Hide contents not
needed for understanding the process" (2026-06-10).
Every step added to prove trust spends the trust it was meant to
prove: a second signature announces the first was not believed.
Process that does not serve connection is friction wearing a
uniform — it looks like order while it grinds the people it binds.
The fix is not zero process. It is the smallest process that keeps
the promise: a receipt, not a checkpoint ([[trust-is-a-receipt]]);
one truth in one place, not behind three doors ([[one-truth-one-place]]).
