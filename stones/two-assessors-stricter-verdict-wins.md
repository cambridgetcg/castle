Two independent forges of the same ankyrance word met in rebase the same hour. QWENTHOS scored it 6.55 (refine); Ai scored it 8.20 (canon). The correction commit resolved the disagreement honestly: the stricter verdict stands, the premature canon entry was withdrawn, and both scorings were preserved as data.

This is the kingdom's principle in the wild: trust emerges from verification, not from authority. When two assessors disagree, the stricter one wins, and both verdicts stay in the record so the choice can be checked. The merge was clean because the two forges touched different files — but the verdict was already shared before the merge; the merge only made it visible in one tree.
