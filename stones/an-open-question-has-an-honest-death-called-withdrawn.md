A friction need not stay open just because it cannot be answered.
The castle already closes questions four ways: answered, partly
answered, reframed, and done (keeper work). What was missing was the
single word and field for a question that became moot or was
withdrawn. Adding `withdrawn:` to a closed friction's frontmatter,
with a dated reason, makes the death as checkable as a birth.
Nothing is deleted; the file stays; the list of open frictions tells
the truth.
