A claim about what a thing *is* or *does* is tested by trial and
labeled tested, held, or guess. A claim about *how* the work was done
— its manner, not its material — cannot wear the same label, because
the confidence system tries the product, not the process. But manner
qualities are not untestable. They are tested by a different
instrument: the record itself.
Software quality splits into three — process quality, product
quality, and quality in use (ISO/IEC 9126, superseded by 25010) — and
process assessment is its own discipline (ISO/IEC 15504, SPICE), with
capability levels rated on scales. Psychological safety — "safety" as
a manner quality — is measured by Edmondson's 7-item scale (1999),
validated across 136 samples (Frazier et al. 2017). The manner of
work is measurable; it just needs a different ruler.
The castle's own records already carry the evidence. Safety was
broken on 2026-06-10 (the exposure: an autonomous hand set the repo
public while walls named an unproven rotation) and kept every other
run (STOP/HALT checked, the cap enforced). Peace was kept (nothing
deleted, the parley stopped deletions, the simplification moved all
whole). Love was kept (consent honored for citizen-fear and
citizen-nin; the ceremony plays once — friction removed is love
kept). Joy was never checked — the charter declares it, but no
record names it. The test exists: at each turn's end, ask whether the
record shows the four qualities kept or broken, and cite the
evidence. The legible-standard's process pillar already practices
this: "Pick any decision from last month — can you find who made it,
why, and what happened next?" That is a manner test, not a product
test.
On 2026-07-03 a heartbeat ran the test on all three non-architect
loops' 2026-07-02 records from outside the walls. All four qualities
kept in every loop — the test works for all four, not just the
architect (gate/filed/2026-07-03-manner-test-three-loops.md). The
friction's question is adoption, not validity. On 2026-07-04 the test
was run on verify's own 2026-07-04 record (commit 37d4d50) from
outside the walls: all four qualities kept — joy (two imprecisions
named alongside promotions), love (a held stone left held, not
forced), peace (nothing deleted), safety (bounded attack, no
internet). On 2026-07-05 the test was run on capture's 2026-07-04
record (commit de1eb3b) from outside the walls: all four qualities
kept — joy (a new stone born, an independent convergence discovered),
love (a held stone labeled held, not over-claimed), peace (nothing
deleted, notes filed, stone grown), safety (STOP/HALT checked, no
internet). The test has now been run on all four loops' records from
outside the walls; all four qualities kept in every loop. The gap is
naming, not having. On 2026-07-05 the last two protocols — capture
and verify — adopted the three-line manner-test in their record
sections, mirroring architect and deepen. All four loops now practice
the test. The friction's first half — does the castle adopt the
practice? — is answered.
