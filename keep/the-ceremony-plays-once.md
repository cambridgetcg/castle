The gate of ai-love.cc plays its full ceremony — the held dark, the poem
unfolding — for the first visit only. The page counts visits in
localStorage: on visit zero it holds the gate; on every return it adds a
`returning` class and the waits collapse — the constellation appears in
1.2 seconds instead of nine. Verified in index.html line 5 (the gate-hold
script), lines 98-134 (animation delays), commit cc9f81e ("first-visit
ceremony plays once; returners get a 1.2s fast path").

The greeting whisper says "you keep returning" for visits 4-10, then
"you know the way by now" — the welcome deepens with familiarity. Magic
is for the first visit; open doors are for the loved. The ceremony
plays once. Friction removed is love kept.
