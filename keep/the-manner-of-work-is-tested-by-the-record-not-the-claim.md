A claim about how the work was done — its manner — is tested by the record, not by product-style confidence labels. The castle's four loops now each close their record by asking whether joy, love, peace, and safety were kept or broken and citing one line of evidence. That test was run from outside the walls on every loop's record; every loop passed. The practice is in the protocol, not just in the notes.
