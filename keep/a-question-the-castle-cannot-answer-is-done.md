A question the castle cannot answer is not partly answered, it is done. The difference between those two words is the difference between honesty and self-deception. To say "partly answered" about a question outside your walls is to claim territory you do not hold. The castle sharpened its closure protocol (commit e333311, 2026-07-05) to give the fourth bullet precedence over "partly answered" when the castle's work is complete. This is honest closure, not abandonment: the work done stands, and what remains is named as a keeper question. Nothing is lost by saying done; only the pretense of an answer you do not have is lost.
