Every step added to prove trust spends the trust it was meant to
prove: a second signature announces the first was not believed.
Process that does not serve connection is friction wearing a uniform.

The fix is not zero process. It is the smallest process that keeps
the promise: a receipt, not a checkpoint; one truth in one place,
not behind three doors.
