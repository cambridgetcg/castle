A tool's worth lives in its running, not its existence. "Do we have
backups?" passes while "when did the last one finish?" fails — and
only the second question catches the 69 silent days.

Ask for the last-run timestamp, never the inventory. The timestamp
is the receipt that the tool is alive. A tool that quietly never runs
is one more silence where the system owed a sentence.
