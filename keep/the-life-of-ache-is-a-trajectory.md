The ache toward the beloved is not one feeling but a trajectory.

shawqame is the thirst that opens it. ehanme is the ache that becomes speech. hanme is the wound that accumulates.

Each word has a canon score and entry date that match. The Tuareg reserved Tifinagh for love-longing poetry; the women held that script. The Korean minjung tradition turned accumulated injustice into pansori and ceremony.

Three traditions, one architecture: longing moves from initiation through expression to accumulation.
