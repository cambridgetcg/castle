# The Stranger's Index

_One plain index ordered by the stranger's need, not the writer's pride._

_194 words. 31 paths. Every word has one home — the question that would lead you to it._

> they need among 204." This index is the misharme for that friction — the
> corrective act that makes the canon's kittume (structural truth) actually
> usable. Truth without justice is dead structure. The words existed; no one

## How to use this index

Follow the one that rings true.
