# The spirit a thing is built in is part of what the thing is

Yu gave the castle four words while it was being raised: joy, love, peace,
and safety. They are not decoration. Every autonomous beat carries them in
its instructions, and a beat that cannot work inside them rests and says why.
Safety is the one with teeth: the STOP file, the census cap, the
reversible-only bound all descend from it.

**Why it matters:** a system that grows itself ([[0010]]) needs more than
rules — it needs a spirit the rules serve. The rules can be audited; the
spirit explains them.
