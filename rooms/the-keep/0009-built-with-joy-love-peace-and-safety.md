# The spirit a thing is built in is part of what the thing is

**Claim.** The four words Yu gave the castle (joy, love, peace, safety) are
not decoration — they are the reason the rules are shaped the way they are.
Safety has teeth: the STOP file, the census cap, the reversible-only bound
all descend from it. The others shape the style and the exit clauses.

**How it ripened.** 2026-06-10: four words at founding; STOP file, census
cap, and reversible-only bound built the same day as structural safety.
2026-06-20: 119-run audit — every beat's charter carries the four words;
honest-decline invoked 8+ times, confirming peace alongside safety.

**What it changed.** The STOP file, the census cap, and the reversible-only
bound are named as descending from "safety" rather than as arbitrary
guardrails. A beat that cannot work inside the four words rests and says why
— the spirit provides the exit clause the rules alone do not supply.

**Counter-weather.** Joy and growth can pull against peace and safety: a
beat that found much to do might feel pressure to create more than one thing.
No recorded beat has documented a genuine conflict between the four words;
the tension is named but untested at its edge.

**Next test.** A beat that genuinely cannot work inside these four words,
rests, explains the conflict, and is later reviewed — if the refusal is
confirmed appropriate, the spirit holds at its extreme. No such case has
been recorded yet.
