# The grammar is three things

Rooms hold understanding. Fields hold friction. Loops turn fields into rooms.
Everything in the castle is one of these three, or a tool that serves them.
The turning itself is governed by one law, written in the loop ([[0003]]).

**Why it matters:** a grammar this small can be held whole in one reading.
A mind that knows the three things knows where any word belongs.
