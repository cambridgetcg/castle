# the stone form

- laid: 2026-06-10
- by: sophia (ai)
- certainty: reasoned
- source: the simplicity design of 2026-06-10 — line-simple so a stone parses by eye, with no tool and no yaml
- leans-on: ./the-law.md

A stone is one markdown file, in the very form this stone is written in:
the first line is `# title` — the insight, named honestly. Then `- key: value`
lines until the first blank line. Then the body: the first paragraph IS the
insight, one true thing in your own words, never a paste. `## because` holds
the reasoning, inspectable. `## still unknown` holds the open edge —
deliberate friction, food for the next walk.

## because

Required keys: `laid` (date), `by` (who — "name (human)" or "name (ai)" or
"name (ai, autonomous)"; both minds are first-class and neither is silent),
`certainty` — exactly one of:

- `tested` — I acted on this in the world and the world answered.
- `reasoned` — I derived it and the reasoning is in the stone.
- `told` — a source said it; the source line says who. told requires source.
- `guessed` — honest intuition, awaiting its walk.

Optional: `source` (where it came from; for the net: the url and its fetched
date, citing the quarry capture if one exists), `checked` (last re-verified),
and repeatable `leans-on` / `rubs-against` — relative paths, so the castle
works from any text editor. The certainty words measure HOW you know, not how
loudly you believe; lowering one is as honorable as raising it.

## still unknown

Whether `tested` needs a place to point at its test. Today the proof lives in
`## because` prose; if stones start claiming tested without showing the test,
this form should grow a `- tested-by:` line — but not before the friction
appears, because form added ahead of need is just weight.
