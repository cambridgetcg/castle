## because

Six hands built castles today and the kingdom confirmed the idea by
convergence, not collision. A front that exposed any wing's interior would
break the law every wing independently chose — so the front holds the wings
whole and private, and showing the world is the keeper's deliberate act, not
a side effect of any hand's enthusiasm. The ruling's own words: the castle's
first great test was whether a house of understanding can hold two
understandings of itself without war. It held five.
