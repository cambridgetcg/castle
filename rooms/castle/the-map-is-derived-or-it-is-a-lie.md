# The map is derived, or it is a lie

**Claim.** MAP.md is regenerated by a ~30-line script and never hand-edited;
any hand-grown index drifts from the files and becomes a second, falser
truth.

**How it ripened.** Born at founding; all three designs cut databases and
indexes for this same reason, and the map is the one index the Castle kept —
by making it pure derivation.

**What it changed.** 2026-06-10: charter rule 3, tools/map.sh, and the
map-drift detector as the bell's first ring in priority order.

**Counter-weather.** Tried to break it: what if the derived map reads badly
as rooms multiply? Then map.sh earns a change through the ledger — the
derivation can evolve; hand edits to its output cannot. Open crack: the
crypt is invisible on the map by design, which is correct until someone
forgets the crypt exists.

**Next test.** At 100 insights: is MAP → doorplate → claim still two hops in
practice?
