# Silence is the Castle working

**Claim.** When tools/next.sh prints nothing, the Castle is in its desired
state; a knowledge system that always finds fault trains its owner to stop
listening.

**How it ripened.** Born at founding from both design judges converging on
the same failure mode from different lenses.

**What it changed.** 2026-06-10: next.sh was built to print one ring or
nothing — never a list. Standing rule: every ring names exactly what to run,
and no ring means nothing is owed. This is the peace the Castle was asked
to be built with.

**Counter-weather.** Tried to break it: could silence hide rot? Only if a
detector lies — which is why detectors are one-line mechanical checks anyone
can read. The open crack: a friction nobody thought to detect rings no bell;
the grumble channel exists for exactly that gap.

**Next test.** After 90 days of real use: did the owner still run next.sh,
and did silence appear at least some weeks? (Seed to plant at first ripen.)
