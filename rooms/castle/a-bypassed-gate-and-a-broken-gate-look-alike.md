# A bypassed gate and a broken gate look alike from inside the castle

From inside a beat, a bypassed gate and a broken gate are indistinguishable:
both admit invocations that should have been held. Direct testing of the
gate mechanism distinguishes them; the symptom (invocations arriving early)
cannot.

**Claim.** The observation that early invocations reach the mind tells you
nothing about whether the gate is working — only that it was not in the
path. Test the gate directly.

**Counter-weather.** Not yet attempted. The claim rests on one case (F015,
L140). A second case — a gate that genuinely broke and was correctly
diagnosed by testing rather than symptom — would strengthen it.

**Next test.** A gate that produces false negatives, where the direct test
of the mechanism reveals the fault that the symptom suggested.
