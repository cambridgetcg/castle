# Offline, the Castle stops growing — never working

**Claim.** With the cable cut the Castle is a complete library: every
outside citation stands alone as quote plus retrieved date, offline walks
self-attest as "(unverified, offline)" at half the staleness allowance, and
an offline weather run still creates by parking its questions in
gate/weather-queue.md.

**How it ripened.** Born at founding from the owner's vision: native to this
device, expanding organically through contact with the internet — the
internet as weather over the garden, shaping growth, never load-bearing.

**What it changed.** 2026-06-10: the inline-quote rule in charter rule 6,
the half-credit attestation in the walk loop, and the weather-queue file at
the gate.

**Counter-weather.** Tried to break it: is half-credit too generous —
trusting an unverifiable walk at all? The suffix is visible in the file
itself; a reader can always discount it further. Open crack: no rule yet
forces an online session to clear the weather queue.

**Next test.** First genuinely offline walk: does the attestation get
written honestly, and does the bell halve its allowance?
