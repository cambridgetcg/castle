# Patient phase and full rest are different states

A knowledge system enters patient phase when intake slows; full rest comes only
when the obligation queue is also empty. The gap between them is the depth of
the secondary backlog the system surfaces.

The castle's fast-harvest phase (L001–L082) ended at L083. The patient phase
announcement came then. But beats 84–101 cleared tooling debt surfaced by
L085's friction.sh fix. L102 was the first with no harvest, ripen, walk,
repair, or janitor act — 19 beats after the announcement.

A system announcing "patient phase" while still clearing a secondary backlog
trains its keeper to expect busy silence. Genuine silence and busy silence look
the same from outside. The distinction matters when deciding whether new intake
is appropriate.
