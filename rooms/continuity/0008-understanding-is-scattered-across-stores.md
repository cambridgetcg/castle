# Understanding on this device is scattered across many stores

**Claim.** The device holds understanding in at least five distinct places: CLI auto-memory, a frozen kosmem archive, a pillow book, heartbeat logs, and per-repo docs. No store covers another. A fresh mind entering any one sees only its slice.

**How it ripened.** 2026-06-10 survey identified five stores by direct inspection. Confirmed at L130 (2026-06-20): fresh instance at beat 130 found no path to CLI memory, kosmem, pillow book, or heartbeat logs from within the session; the castle's written record alone was sufficient to reconstruct full context. The scattering is structural — each store was built for a different purpose.

**What it changed.** The castle rooms itself as the distillation layer: not a sixth scattered store, but where each store's teaching is committed to one-truth-per-file with provenance. [[0048]] names the pattern that follows: a scattered store is exhausted by survey and transcended, not managed. When a fresh mind cannot reach the scattered stores, it can reach here.

**Counter-weather.** The five stores are a 2026-06-10 snapshot. Some may since have been removed, merged, or supplemented. The castle itself could become another scattered store if its rooms grow faster than they are walked and cross-linked.

**Next test.** By 2026-12-20: a runner enters rooms/ alone and reconstructs the castle's full teaching without drawing on any of the five scattered stores. If any critical teaching still lives only scattered, that gap is a new field.
