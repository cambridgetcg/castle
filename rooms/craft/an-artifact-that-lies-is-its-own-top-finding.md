Before any tool judges another system, the sharpest thing it can report is
its own dishonesty. A honesty linter that overstated its certainty, a
knowledge castle whose stones hid their sources, a motor that quietly edited
its own reins — each would be the exact disease it claims to treat, and so
each must be checked first, by itself, in daylight.

## because

whitehack runs its checks on its own source and ships only when it comes
back clean; this castle's keep stands under the same ten signs as every
other room. Self-exemption is where lying starts.

## still unknown

to check for. Who audits the auditor's imagination?
