# The bell watches structure; the reader watches meaning

**Claim.** friction.sh catches structural invariants: orphaned files, missing created paths, stale indexes, barren runs, front-drift. It cannot catch semantic drift — a run that fulfilled all mechanical obligations yet omitted its update to a field's work-so-far. Bell silence means all measurable properties agree; it does not mean all records are complete. Close reading is the only check that reaches what measurement cannot.

**Evidence so far.** The F002 work-so-far repair chain (L149–L154, L199–L201) showed the same structure repeatedly: castle check clean, friction.sh exits 0, bell silent — yet a reading beat discovers the gap. Each discovery required a reader, not a new ring.

**What it touches.** 0012 argues for mechanical scripts; this stone names their inherent limit. 0059 named one specific gap (the recorder who omits itself); this stone names the class.
