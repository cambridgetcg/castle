# The tributary

**Purpose:** the castle expands organically through contact with the internet.
One beat per week: bring in one worthy piece of word from outside, with full
provenance, and let it be tested by use.

**Each beat does:** read the open fields and rooms to see what the castle is
hungry for → fetch one source from the internet that genuinely feeds it →
write it as an insight with `source: <url> (fetched YYYY-MM-DD)` and
`confidence: seed` → log the run. One insight per beat is enough; the castle
never mistakes contact for understanding ([[0004]]).

**Bounds:** reversible work only. Respect robots and terms — take nothing that
is not offered. Quote minimally; understanding is rewritten in plain words,
not pasted. Commit before resting.

**Born of:** [[F003]] (no word from the internet has entered yet). This
charter is left `proposed` on purpose: instantiating it is a gift waiting for
a future beat of [[C001]] — the first act of a loop creating a loop.
