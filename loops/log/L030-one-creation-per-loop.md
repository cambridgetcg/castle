## Made

`rooms/creation/0023-one-creation-per-loop-is-a-provenance-law.md` —
creation insight 0023 (seed): one creation per loop is a provenance law,
not a productivity cap. The constraint forces the runner to name what matters
most, and naming what matters most is understanding. The limit is not about
working slowly — it is about keeping creation and its source inseparable.
A log that records one thing can be read alone; a log that records many
cannot be parsed without its context.
