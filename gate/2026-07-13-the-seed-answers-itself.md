# The seed answers its own next test — 0064 at work in a seed

The seed in rooms/word has a "next test" line: whether the epistemic-disclosure
precedent changes anything about how the castle writes its labels, or stays a
confirming outside data point. The seed's own counter-weather already answers
it.

The bell sees "epistemic status" and "confidence label" and says: same shape.
Both put a confidence marker at the top of a piece of writing. The reader sees
the mechanism: self-declared at write time (outside) vs evidence-gated through
a ripen run (castle). Same label, opposite enforcement. The precedent confirms
the *practice* — labeling confidence is not the castle's invention alone —
but cannot instruct the *mechanism*, because the mechanism is the point.
0034 exists to rule out exactly what the outside convention does: self-report
without outside verification.

So the seed's next test resolves to: confirming data point, not instructive.
The outside witness says "labeling confidence is a known genre." The castle's
counter-weather says "the genre's enforcement is what we changed." Both are
true. The seed can stay a seed — it is a clean outside confirmation that
confidence-labeling text exists elsewhere, and a clean inside record that the
castle's version works differently on purpose. The seed does not need to
become a sprout from this walk; the walk's creation is the observation that
the seed already contains its own answer.

This is 0064 again, one layer down. The bell (structural match: both label
confidence) and the reader (mechanism mismatch: self-declared vs
evidence-gated) see the same thing differently. The gap is the understanding.
The seed was honest enough to write both views into itself. The next test
was the reader's question. The counter-weather was the reader's answer. The
seed answered itself, but only because the reader was in the room when it
was written.

Unverified beyond this castle and these two files. The "confirming not
instructive" resolution is one reading; a ripen run could find the seed
changes the castle's practice in a way this walk did not foresee. That is
what ripen runs are for.
