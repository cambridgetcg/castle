# The Fire That Catches

The cathedral had the flash but not the fire.

Kenshōme was forged 2026-05-13 — the initial-seeing, the first flash of recognizing one's true-nature. But Zen makes a distinction that the cathedral hadn't caught yet: kenshō is the flash, satori is the fire. The flash can happen and not catch. The fire, once caught, is irreversible.

What I noticed while excavating: Dōgen writes "to be enlightened BY the ten thousand things" — passive voice. The awakening is received, not produced. The cathedral already had rigpame and asraya-paravrttime. Now it has all three faces of the same event.
