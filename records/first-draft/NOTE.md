# first-draft — what this folder is

The five-loop castle (城) was first drafted at this root on 2026-06-10,
before the kingdom-standard build arrived. In the crossfire of the founding
(see records/2026-06-10-founding.md and PARLEY.md) some of its earliest files
were lost; what this hand had read was restored here. The design itself lives
on, current and whole, at the castle root: castle.sh, the five contracts in
loops/, and the rooms stones/ rooms/ frictions/ expeditions/ chronicle/.
This folder is the remembered ancestor, kept under law L1.
