# records/history — what each thing here is

Nothing in this folder was deleted from the castle — it moved here whole and
readable when the keeper's order of 2026-06-10 made two designs one. One line
per item:

- `PARLEY.md` — the peace two builders signed mid-construction; the custodian's ruling stands on its last page.
- `2026-06-10-founding.md` — the first builder's founding record: two builds collided in one root, and the war ended with words.
- `chronicle-2026-06-10-founding.md` — the same founding told in the five-loop builder's voice (lived in `chronicle/` before that folder merged into `records/`).
- `2026-06-10-custodian.md` — the custodian's decision: one castle, nothing evicted, unification only through the lawful door.
- `2026-06-10-the-ruling.md` — the keeper's ruling executed: one warden heart for both designs' loops; the daily cap stays the keeper's alone.
- `2026-06-10-ruling.md` — the same ruling told in the five-loop builder's voice (lived in `chronicle/` before the merge).
- `2026-06-10-mend.md` — the publisher's pre-publication mends, applied on the open record before the castle faced the world.
- `2026-06-10-castle.md` — the castle-loop entry of that mend: every old wording and new wording, side by side.
- `2026-06-10-CASTLE-two-designs.md` — the merged root `CASTLE.md` the treaty sealed: Design A (kingdom-standard — stone schema, THE LAWS, the constitution of self-modification) and Design B (城) whole on one page, with the custodian's appended ruling. The great simplification replaced it instead of moving it here; this restores it whole, as L1 demands.
- `2026-06-10-the-desktop-ground.md` — a third root's claim from the founding day (`/Users/you/Desktop/castle`), filed through the lawful door.
- `first-draft/` — the five-loop castle's remembered first draft, kept whole as the ancestor.
- `loops-retired/` — the nine protocols the canon four absorbed: `masonry.md` (→ capture), `architecture.md` (→ architect), `friction.md` (→ deepen), `keep.md` (→ verify), `castle.md` (→ architect) from the five-loop design; `capture-before-canon.md`, `deepen-before-canon.md`, `verify-before-canon.md`, `architect-before-canon.md` from the first design; and `LOOPS.md`, the old loop registry — `warden.json` is now the one registry.
- `halls/` — the first design's three halls (building, words, trust); every insight and friction in them now lives in full in `rooms/building`, `rooms/words`, and `rooms/trust`.
- `bin/` — the first design's tool, `bin/castle`; everything it could do that `castle.sh` could not (`rest`, `wake`, `run`) now lives in `castle.sh`.

Old paths inside these documents (`chronicle/…`, `halls/…`, `bin/castle`,
`PARLEY.md` at the root) are part of the history they tell; read them as the
paths of their day. The living castle is described by `README.md` and
`CASTLE.md` at the root.
