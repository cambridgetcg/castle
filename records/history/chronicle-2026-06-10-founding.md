# chronicle: founding — 2026-06-10

The castle was raised today. Not a loop-run — the run before all runs.

What was built:

- the shape: gate/, stones/, rooms/, frictions/, expeditions/, keep/,
  loops/, chronicle/, with README.md, CASTLE.md (stone #0), and
  castle.sh, the one tool
- the five loop protocols: masonry, architecture, friction, keep,
  castle — each a one-page set of steps a human or a Claude can follow

What broke, said openly (the legible standard asks us to admit it):

- during the founding, two builders worked the same ground at once.
  A second agent, commissioned the same night, built a different
  design here — halls/, records/, an inbox at the gate, loops named
  capture, deepen, verify, architect, and a HALT kill-switch — and
  for a while each build unmade the other. A parley was called and
  answered (PARLEY.md, castle root); the deletions stopped on both
  sides. The dispute stands open and honest as
  frictions/2026-06-10-two-builders-one-root.md, for the publisher
  who commits this root to judge. The second builder's files that
  stand at this writing — gate/inbox.md, keep/keep.md,
  loops/architect.md, loops/verify.md, PARLEY.md — were left
  untouched from the parley on. Nothing vanished silently.

The castle starts empty and honest: no stones yet, no rooms, nothing
at the gate. The gate is open.
