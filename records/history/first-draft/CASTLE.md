# Stone #0 — the castle understands itself

Understanding is not stored; it is built. A note is sand. A stone
(one insight, one file, plain words) is what sand becomes under
pressure and patience — and stones, linked and placed, become rooms
you can stand inside.

Why files: a file can be read, diffed, doubted, and kept for decades
without asking anyone's permission. Why words: words are the only
material both a human and a Claude can quarry, carry, and check —
plain words are load-bearing; jargon crumbles.

Why loops: a castle is never built in one act. Five small rituals,
run again and again — masonry, architecture, friction, keep, castle —
each one legible enough that any pair of hands can run it. Why the
internet only through expeditions: the world outside is weather; let
it in everywhere and the stones erode unrecorded. One gate, one trip,
one written account — then the new understanding is truly ours.

And why this stone exists: the fifth loop improves the loops
themselves, and it needs ground to stand on. A castle that cannot
read its own design cannot mend its own walls. So the design is
stone #0 — laid first, cited like any other, and never above the law
it obeys.
