# The Castle

Understanding, kept: plain markdown on this machine, one true thing per
file, each wearing an honest label of how sure it is. Founded 2026-06-10
with joy, love, peace and safety. Several hands build here; where two
grammars disagree, fields/F005 holds the peace — you need none of that to
start.

## The three moves

1. **Save a thought.** Write it, verbatim, into `gate/2026-06-10-few-words.md`.
   Done. This is the only ceremony the castle ever requires of you.
2. **Ask the bell.** `sh tools/next.sh` prints the one next action — or
   nothing. Nothing means all is well and you owe the castle nothing today.
3. **Tend.** When the bell names a loop, open that file in `loops/`, do what
   it says, and note what you made in `ledger/`.

The tending also runs itself: `tools/tend.sh`, on a schedule, answers one
ring a day and signs the ledger like anyone else. Silence costs nothing.

## How sure is a stone

`seed` (kept, no evidence yet) → `sprout` (one piece of evidence) →
`tested` (two independent pieces, a recorded attempt to break it, and what
it changed) → `cornerstone` (three pieces, one from outside, survived a
re-read after day 90). A contradiction moves a stone down one rung,
immediately. Labels move only in recorded ripen runs, and demotion is
written as plainly as promotion — inflation is the only sin.

## The shape

    gate/      thoughts land here, verbatim — the one required ceremony
    rooms/     one insight per file; the filename is the claim
    loops/     the chores, written down; LOOM.md holds the law and templates
    tools/     the bell, the door, the map, the circle, the front
    ledger/    what every run made — append-only honesty
    crypt/     nothing is deleted; moved things forward from here forever

## The few promises

Nothing enters carelessly. Nothing is deleted. Every run is recorded. No
label lies. No tool fetches the web — the one exception: tend.sh invites an
agent via the claude CLI; remove its schedule to sever it. A stone reaches
the public front only when marked `front: public` and carried through by a
recorded run.

Stone and ledger templates, and the finer law: `loops/LOOM.md`.
For agents: `CLAUDE.md`. The map of what is known: `MAP.md`.
